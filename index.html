CHEST

Building a strong chest is essential for overall upper body strength. Our chest exercises include:
  • Bench Press: Targets the pectoralis major muscle
  • Dumbbell Press: Works the chest muscles all-round
  • Incline Press: Focuses on the upper chest muscles

ABS

A strong core is the foundation of a healthy and athletic body. Our ABs exercises include:
  • Crunches: Targets the rectus abdominis muscle
  • Leg Raises: Works the lower abs
  • Planks: Engages the entire core muscle group

LEGS

Building strong legs is crucial for overall lower body strength. Our leg exercises include:
  • Squats: Targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes
  • Lunges: Works the legs and glutes all-round
